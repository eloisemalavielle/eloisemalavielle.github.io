Patouë
N* 1 : Le Berrichon
N* 2 : Le Ch´Ti

folded format (7 x 9) cm
unfolded format (18,5 x 26,5) cm

Periodical typographic fanzine on French patois, a non-official language sometimes considered coarse and seldom explained to the last generations. I wanted to face this lack, in a playful and accessible way by playing with various typefaces and making these fanzines available mostly in cultural places and school.
