Les Pokémons
envahissent l´art

Acrylics on canvas
Series in progress

The aim of this series is to distort the most famous paintings (artistic culture) by incorporating a Pokemon (pop culture) in link with the painting’s composition and thus create an visual oxymoron while learning closer the appropraited artist’s strokes.
