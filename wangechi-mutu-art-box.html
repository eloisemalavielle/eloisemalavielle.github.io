Wangechi Mutu, contemporary artist, her art box

box : assembled : 30 x 23 x 14,5 cm,
editions : 3 (27,5 x 20 cm) + 18,5 x 12,3 cm artsitic references
posters 1, 2, 3, editions (40 x 60) cm
calligraphic research with the artist’s favorite medium : ink

Creation of an editorial and sculptural object, thanks to the box who contained the editions and posters but can also be built as two displays for a reading time.

Based on my research on the Kenyan artist’s work I divise it in three axis :

1) Fuse : creatures mades of paper and inks

2) Eve : the restyled figure

3) Bronze : myths in kind
